## About me

I'm a Full Stack Developer based in Balneário Camboriú, Brazil, with a focus on backend architecture, AI agents, and large-scale automation. I've been coding since I was 15 and have been working professionally for over a year — building systems that go well beyond typical CRUD applications.

In my current role, I designed and maintain a complete ecosystem of AI agents running in production (WhatsApp, CRM, appointment scheduling), 30+ automation workflows operating 24/7, and contributed to building an internal multi-tenant SaaS platform that replaced multiple third-party tools.

I'm also the founder of [CVEngine](https://cvengine.com.br) — an AI-powered SaaS that analyzes job descriptions, scores profile compatibility, and generates ATS-optimized resumes tailored to each specific role.
